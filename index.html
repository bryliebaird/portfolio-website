About

“If you don’t like your destiny, don’t accept it. Instead have the courage to change it the way you want it to be.”

My passion for creating and taking on complex challenges has led me to a career change in web development. As a former teacher, I found myself drawn to creating engaging activities for students using technology. From designing pixel art puzzles using Google Sheets to developing digital escape room challenges, I knew I needed to pursue this calling.

I’ve had the opportunity to complete a Coding Bootcamp at Austin Community College and have since then continued to develop my skills through various online courses. I’m excited to be able to fully transition into this field and look forward to continuing to refine my skills with the right group of people.
